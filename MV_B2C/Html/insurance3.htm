Brief Description of Insurance Coverage
Majestic Vacations has selected AIG Travel Guard to provide travel insurance coverage and assistance for our customers and their families. American International Group (AIG) is one of the largest insurance and assistance providers in the world, and AIG Travel Guard is one of the leading travel insurance providers in North America.

The following plans are available through Majestic Vacations :

Individual & Group Inclusive Tour Program (P1)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Tour Cost Trip Cancellation & 125% of Tour Cost Trip Interruption
Reimburses for forfeited, nonrefundable, unused payments or deposits if you must cancel or interrupt your trip due to a covered illness, injury, or death of you, an immediate family member, or traveling companion or for other covered reasons.

$500 Travel Delay
Reimburses up to $100 per day if your trip is delayed for more than 12 hours for reasonable additional expenses until travel becomes possible.

$1,000 Baggage & Personal Effects
Reimburses you if your luggage is lost, damaged, or stolen while you are on your trip.

$100 Baggage Delay
Reimburses you for the purchase of essential items if your bags are delayed for more than 24 hours.

$20,000 Medical Expense
Covers necessary medical expenses up to one year after the sickness or injury provided you sought initial medical treatment while on your trip.

$20,000 Emergency Medical Transportation
Covers evacuation and transportation to the nearest adequate medical facility.

$25,000 Accidental Death & Dismemberment
Covers death and loss of limb or eyesight within 180 days of an accident.

AIG Travel Assist
Offers emergency assistance for anywhere in the world.

LiveTravel®
24-hour hotline to make emergency travel changes, such as rebooking flights, hotel reservations, tracking lost luggage and more!

Emergency Cash transfers
Assistance in coordinating an emergency cash advance.

Pre-trip travel advisories
Around-the-clock access to passport, visa, inoculation and vaccine requirements; travel advisories; embassy and consulate contacts; travel health advisories; weather and currency information - all for your planned destination.

As a special added coverage; if you purchase and pay for this plan along with your first payment (deposit or full payment) you will automatically receive Coverage for pre-existing medical conditions.
* Not all travel insurance policies are the same!
