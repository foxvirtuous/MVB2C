Visa Overview: China

Visitor:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Visa is required for U.S. citizens visiting P.R. China.

How to apply:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. You must come to the visa office of the Embassy or Consulate -General in the consular jurisdiction in which you live to submit the application; or
2. If you cannot come personally, you may entrust Majestic Vacations to come to the visa office of the Embassy or Consulate -General in the consular jurisdiction in which you live for the application process.

Requirements:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Your valid & actual passport which must have at least six (6) months of remaining validity with at least one blank visa page in it.
2. One completed Visa Application Form (Q1) with one passport photo (black & white or color is acceptable).

Processing time (Mail / Deliver Time was NOT included):
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The regular processing time is 4 working days. 1 working day and 2-3 working days express service is available upon request and is subject to additional fee.

Visa Validity and Duration of Stay:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Usually the validity of a Single Entry or Double Entry "L" visa is 90 days or 180 days from the date of issue. This means the holder of the visa shall enter China no later than 90 days or 180 days from the date of issue, otherwise the visa is expired and is null and void. The duration of stay of a "L" visa is 30 days, which means the holder of the visa may stay in China for up to 30 days from the date of entry.
2. The visa officer may extend the Duration of Stay if the applicant needs and requests a stay in China for more than 30 days.

Additional information:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Any person suffering from a mental disorder, leprosy, AIDS, venereal diseases, contagious tuberculosis or other such infectious diseases shall not be permitted to enter China.
