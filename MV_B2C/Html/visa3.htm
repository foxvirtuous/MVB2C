Visa Overview: Singapore

Visitor:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Foreigners holding travel documents issued by different countries will require either a Business or Social Visit visa prior to entry into Singapore.

• U.S. citizens:
Please refer to your enquiry regarding a Singapore visa. The holder of a United States passport, with a validity of six months or longer, does not require a visa to visit Singapore as a tourist, for business or for social visit purposes. The visitor must be in possession of a confirm onward or return air ticket, sufficient funds and entry facilities to the visitor's ultimate destination. The period of stay permitted is at the discretion of the immigration officer in Singapore and is normally for 30 days.

• China passport holders

Requirements: (Send the following items to any of your local Majestic Vacation office)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Two Visa Application Forms:
Print out Visa Applicaiton form provide on the right side of this page, fill out completed, signed, and dated. Please provide day time contact phone number.
• Passport:
Original passport and two photocopies of Passport (from Page 1 to passport validity date, including the US visa page). Passport must be valid for at least 6 months beyond the last day of intended stay in Singapore.
• Photo:
2 recent color passport photos.
Must be two-inch half length, bareheaded and full-faced photo. No laser photos are accepted.
• Air Ticket:
A photocopy of confirmed onward AIR-TICKET. Itineraries and open tickets are not acceptable. A valid visa for the next destination should be obtained (if applicable).
• Copy of US Resident Alien / PR /Green Card (if applicable).
• If you are returning to USA after visiting Singapore, please produce a valid US visa or relevant documentary proof confirming your returnability, e.g. INS Parole Paper, etc.
• Cost:
Payment may different due to the postage. Contact your local Majestic Vacation office for the total. If you pay by check, make check payable to "Majestic Vacations".

Additional comments:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Original supporting documents will be returned immediately after checking at the submission counter.
