Visa Overview: Vietnam

Visitors:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Any foreigner or overseas Vietnamese wishing to visit Vietnam must fill out one entry visa application form. Applied up to six months prior to the date of travel.

Requirements: (Send the following items to any of your local Majestic Vacation office)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• One Visa Application Form:
Print out Visa Applicaiton form provide on the right side of this page, fill out completed, signed, and dated.
• Passport: Original passport.
• Photo: 4cm by 6cm (2"x2")
Two for overseas Vietnamese born in Vietnam; one for all other foreigners. The pictures should be of the head and shoulders only, taken without a hat. These pictures have to be no older than a year before the date of filing application.
• Cost:
Payment may different due to the postage. Contact your local Majestic Vacation office for the total. If you pay by check, make check payable to "Majestic Vacations".

Additional comments:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Five business days are required to process normal visa applications.
2. Expedited processing requires two business days after the Embassy has received the application (additional fee required).
