Instructions For Using Our Service:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Print and complete our visa checklist. Click here to download
2. Print and fill out the visa application form (Form Q-1) for each person. This form must be completed in full including the intended date of entry into China. Click here to download PDF file
3. Include one passport-sized photograph (black & white or color) with each visa application form.
4. Make sure that your passport has at least 6 months validity from the intended date of entry into China. (Most Asian countries require that passport be valid for at least 6 months after entry)
5. Mail us 1 completed visa checklist, a visa application form for each person with photo, a passport for each person, and a check for the appropriate fee. (Checks should be made payable to Majestic Vacations). Our offices near you
6. Send an email to visa@majestic-vacations.com to inform us that you have mailed your documents to us. Please include tracking number if you are sending to us by Fed Ex.
