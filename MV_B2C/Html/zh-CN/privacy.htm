What Are Cookies And Do We Use Them?
Cookies are a standard method of registering user preferences when people use web sites, to ensure a better and more personalized experience. They are pieces of information stored on your computer's hard disk drive. This ensures that we know information about you when you next visit our site. By using a cookie when you return, you will not have to enter it all again. At present the cookies we use assist you in compiling a list of your preferred hotels when browsing the site and they assist us in measuring the success of our advertising. The cookies we pass on to you are anonymous and do not hold information on you or reveal your personal identity.
