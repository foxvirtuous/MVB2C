Day 1
Depart your hometown for your adventure to Brazil!

Day 2
Upon arrival, enjoy the rest of the day to relax! Overnight in Rio de Janeiro.

Day 3
Today, after a hearty breakfast, enjoy a complete panoramic city tour with lunch. Overnight: Enjoy a wonderful supper in Rio!

Day 4 / 5
After your next tropical breakfast, take advantage of two free days in exciting Rio! Overnight in Rio de Janeiro.

Day 6
After your last tropical breakfast Rio style, transfer to the airport for your flight to Brazil's first capital, Salvador. Upon arrival in Salvador, you will be transferred to your chosen hotel and given an orientation brief of the many things you can do in this exciting city that has been called by UNESCO a world heritage site for its historical center and cultural heritage. Balance of the day at leisure. Overnight in Salvador.

Day 7
After your breakfast at your hotel, embark on a complete morning half day historical tour of Salvador. Overnight in Salvador.

Day 8
After a hearty tropical breakfast, “Bahiano style”, the day will be yours at leisure for optional tours. Overnight in Salvador.

Day 9 / 10
After your last tropical style breakfast, transfer to the airport for your home-bound flight, which arrives on the next day.
