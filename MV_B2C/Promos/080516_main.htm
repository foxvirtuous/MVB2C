The Caribbean Princess Hotel
Our Rating:
A beautiful four-star resort situated between the Caribbean and the Nichupte Lagoon, is close to Cancún's center and shopping centers. Specializes mainly in all-inclusive packages. The hotel's beautiful architecture and Mediterranean style decor invites you to enjoy the warm, pleasant weather.
